About Me

Hello, I'm Aashutosh, an MSCS student at Georgia Tech, specialising in Machine Learning. I'm passionate about Applied ML, Multimodal LLMs and Generative AI and I love exploring how cutting-edge AI can be applied to build impactful, real-world systems.

I was previously a research intern at Microsoft Research India, working on Applied ML for large-scale NoSQL Database Systems. Before that I graduated with distinction from BITS Pilani, with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.

My research interests lie at the intersection of ML systems, Generative Modelling and Multimodal Intelligence. I have previously published research on Multimodal LLMs and Generative AI, and have active research on VLMs.

In my free time, I enjoy competitive programming, watching tennis and playing pool.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss ideas, potential collaborations, or research opportunities.
