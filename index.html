About Me

Hello, I'm Aashutosh, an MSCS student at Georgia Tech, specialising in Machine Learning. I'm passionate about Applied ML, Multimodal Learning and Generative AI and I love exploring how cutting-edge AI can be applied to build impactful, real-world systems.

I work as a GRA with Dr. Jonna Lee, and as a Graduate Researcher with Dr. James Hays at Georgia Tech. Previously, I was a research intern at Microsoft Research India, working on Applied ML for Optimising NoSQL Workloads. Before that I graduated with distinction from BITS Pilani, with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.

My research interests lie at the intersection of ML systems, Generative Modelling and Multimodal Intelligence. I have previously published research on Generative AI and Computer Vision, and have active research on VLMs.

In my free time, I enjoy competitive programming(I'm a Candidate Master on Codeforces), watching tennis and playing pool.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss ideas, potential collaborations, or research opportunities.
